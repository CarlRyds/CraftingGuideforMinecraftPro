powered rails





Description

A powered rail is a type of rail that is used to decrease or increase the momentum of moving minecarts. Powered rails can be crafted and previously-placed powered rails can be broken with bare hands or any tool, dropping themselves as items. To remove a powered rail, attack it. A powered rail can be used as a rail and as a redstone component, they can be powered by a redstone torch or lever.





ingredients

1x stick, 1x redstone,
6x gold ingot



Gives you...

6x powered rails
