minecart with hopper





Description

A Minecart with Hopper is a hopper inside a minecart. Minecarts with hoppers can be broken by attacking them. These minecarts are placed similarly to other minecarts. A Minecart with hopper will take in items which are lying nearby (range only slightly larger than the cart itself), or inside a container above the track (aprox. 18.5 items per second). It will also pick up items that are lying on a block directly above the track. It will not push items into containers, but a hopper underneath the track can take items out of it. The hopper can be disabled by passing over a powered activator rail, and can be reenabled by an inactive activator rail.
