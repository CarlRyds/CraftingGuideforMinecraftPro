food

salmon





Description

Raw salmon is a food item that is obtained by fishing. It can be used to tame ocelots. The most common way of obtaining raw salmon, like any other fish, is fishing. The chance of one being caught can be increased with the 'Lure' enchantment. One raw salmon restores 2 hunger points and 0.4 saturation. Although it is more efficient to cook it in a furnace to produce cooked salmon, which restores 6 hunger points and 9.6 saturation.





ingredients

raw salmon + coal in a furnace



Gives you...

1x cooked salmon
