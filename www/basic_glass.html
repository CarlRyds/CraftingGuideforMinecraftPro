basic items

glass





Description

Glass is a decorative fully transparent block. Glass will only drop itself if it is broken with a tool enchanted with silk touch; otherwise it will drop nothing. Most blocks dependent on a block cannot be placed on glass. The only exceptions are torches and redstone torches, which can be placed on top of glass, but not attached to their sides.





ingredients (in a furnace)

1x sand
1x fuel



Gives you...

1x glass
