tools

axe





Description

Axes are tools used to ease the process of collecting wood based items. The axe is used to break wooden items faster. An axe can also be used as a weapon, in which role it deals 1 heart less damage than a sword made of the same material. Usage of the axe as a weapon will deal double durability damage to the axe.





ingredients

2x sticks
3x cobblestone (or wood planks, iron, etc)



Gives you...

1x axe
